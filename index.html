Everything You Need to Know About CCTV Installation in Ethiopia

What is the installation process for CCTV?
The CCTV installation process starts with a site assessment where our expert technicians evaluate your property to determine ideal camera positions. Next, we plan the system layout, run the necessary cables, install cameras and recorders, configure your network settings for remote viewing, and test the system thoroughly. At AtoZe Engineering, we handle everything from start to finish — efficiently and professionally.

How many cameras do I need?
The number of cameras you need depends on your property size, security objectives, and blind spots. A small home may need 2–4 cameras, while commercial premises often require 8 or more for full coverage. We offer free assessments to recommend the right number of cameras for maximum protection without overpaying.

Can I use CCTV cameras at night?
Absolutely. Most modern CCTV cameras come equipped with infrared (IR) night vision or low-light sensors that allow clear recording even in total darkness. We install advanced models that ensure 24/7 visibility — so your security doesn’t stop when the sun goes down.

What is motion detection in CCTV cameras?
Motion detection allows your CCTV system to automatically record or alert you when movement is detected in a monitored area. This smart feature reduces storage usage and helps catch unusual activity without having to review hours of footage. At AtoZe, we configure motion zones to reduce false alarms and keep you informed of real threats.

🛡 Want smarter security?
Call AtoZe Engineering and ask about motion-detecting CCTV options.
Is there maintenance required for CCTV systems?
Yes — just like any technology, routine maintenance ensures your CCTV system works reliably over time. This includes cleaning lenses, checking cable integrity, updating firmware, and testing recording quality. At AtoZe Engineering, we offer affordable maintenance packages to keep your system at peak performance.

🧰 Keep your system running like new
Call now to schedule professional CCTV maintenance.
Are CCTV cameras weatherproof?
Yes! We provide weatherproof and dustproof cameras built to withstand Ethiopia’s climate — from hot sun to heavy rain. Our outdoor cameras are IP-rated and designed for harsh environments, ensuring your system remains reliable no matter the conditions.
